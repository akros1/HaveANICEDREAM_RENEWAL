About


The International Blockchain Evaluation Forum (IBEForum) has officially launched and participants include Blockchain experts from all walks of life and around the world.

The IBEForum, which will be officially launched in June, will be made up of Blockchain experts from various countries.

Namely, Professor Park Su-yong of Sogang University, a Blockchain expert in Korea, Dr. Cho Byung-Wan Hanyang University, recognized as the best in the world in the field of industrial revolution, and Dr. Jochen Biedermann of Germany and other experts from Asia, Europe, and North America have already been agreed to participate in this program.

The IBEForum will also present policies regarding the directions for each country to adapt and adjust to the forthcoming Industrial Revolution and the changes that the Blockchain will bring, as well as addressing the standards and alternatives to decrease risks and scams that exist in the current cryptocurrency market.

There is a reason why the whole world is paying attention to the Blockchain system. However, despite its advantages, the world is also witnessing the negative aspects as well. With the fourth industrial revolution, IBEForum will maximize the positive impact of the Blockchain.

We will try to minimize the side effects in the current market, full of uncertainties and scams by creating a set of standards and guidelines.

When the IBEForum and Jury Online's evaluation system work hand in hand, the synergy effect will be maximized."

The International Blockchain Verification Evaluation Forum will be officially launched in June.

Our Goal


We will maximize the positive impact of the Blockchain, and try to minimize the side effects in the current market, full of uncertainties and scams by creating a set of standards and guidelines.
